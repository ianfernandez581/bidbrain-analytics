Reddit - community awareness deep-dive · whole-flight summary

Reddit delivery efficiency

Impressions (bars) against CPM (cost per 1,000 impressions) and CPC (cost per click). Reddit is a top-of-funnel awareness play into a niche AU tech audience - as the always-on flight matures, impressions taper while CPM/CPC climb (the rising cost of a narrow auction), even though click-through holds. That cost trend - not performance - is why raw impression volume falls. Campaign filter + date range apply.
