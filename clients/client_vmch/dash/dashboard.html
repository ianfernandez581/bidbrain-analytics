Media → Traffic — what the display flight delivered

The full chain from ad spend to outcomes: reach, clicks to site, and the post-view and post-click conversions The Trade Desk attributes back to the ads. Programmatic display is an upper-funnel, awareness & retargeting channel, so GA4 records few last-click "Display" sessions by design — the fair read is reach plus ad-attributed conversions, not a one-to-one last-click session lift. Use the Campaign filter to focus the media side.
