Display advertising & the VMCH website

Programmatic display advertising (four service-line campaigns — Residential Aged Care, Retirement Living, Seniors at Home, Disability) set against what happened on the VMCH website in Google Analytics 4. The view defaults to the full available history so the trend is visible; the live campaign flight runs from April 2026 and is marked on every trend chart. Reporting currency AUD. VMCH is a not-for-profit, so website outcomes are enquiries — phone calls, emails and contact forms — plus ad-attributed conversions, not revenue.

Website traffic — GA4

The VMCH website (one GA4 property). This is the outcome the media is working toward: visits, engagement and enquiries (phone, email, contact forms). The sessions trend runs the full selected range (flight marked); KPI cards and the enquiry chart are scoped to the flight (Apr 2026 →), the window where enquiry tagging is comparable.
