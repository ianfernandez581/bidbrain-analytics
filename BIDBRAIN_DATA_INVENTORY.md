# Bidbrain data inventory — 2026-07-06

Generated by Claude Code audit. Read-only snapshot of the `bidbrain-analytics` BigQuery project
(region `australia-southeast1`). All figures pulled live via `bq` (each query capped at 1 GB scanned).

## Executive summary

The pipeline holds **37.83 GB across 20 datasets** — but that number is dominated by one asset:
`raw_google_ads` (36 GB) which carries **Google Ads history back to 2018** via native BigQuery Data
Transfer. Everything a dashboard shows lives in the **five shared `raw_*` datasets**; the eleven
`client_<c>` datasets are almost entirely **views** (0 MB) over that raw layer, plus a handful of tiny
seed tables. Four datasets are **empty** (`billing_export`, `client_bellshakespeare`, `client_caltex`,
`client_nextsmile`).

The single most important finding for planning: **history depth is wildly uneven by source and client.**
The DTS-native sources are deep — Google Ads to 2018 (91 months for City Perfume), GA4 to 2022
(27–49 months), and City Perfume's Neto e-commerce revenue back to 2021 (60 months). But the
**programmatic/paid layer is shallow**: the Snowflake Trade Desk mirror only starts **2026-03-29**
(2–4 months per client), Reddit and Salesforce content-syndication start 2026-03/04, and LinkedIn/DV360
go back to mid-2025 (~13 months) but are split across currency-suffixed accounts that start and stop.

Net: **no client has 24 months across 3+ paid channels.** Only **City Perfume** clears the MMM bar
(deep Google Ads + GA4 + 5 years of revenue), and even it has real paid-channel depth on Google Ads
only. **ResetData** — the proposed V4 recommendations case study — has the broadest *live* channel mix
(Google/Meta/Reddit/Trade Desk/GA4 + a HubSpot funnel) but only 3–7 months of history, which is fine
for a recommendations engine and far too little for MMM.

## Per-client profiles

Verdict rule: **MMM-READY** = ≥24 months across ≥3 channels · **MMM-BORDERLINE** = 12–23 months ·
**MMM-INSUFFICIENT** = <12 months (on paid channels). "Channels" counts paid media + revenue/outcome
sources; GA4 alone is an outcome layer, not a paid channel.

### City Perfume  (100% Digital · e-commerce)
- Datasets: `client_cityperfume` (37 views) · sources in `raw_google_ads`, `raw_ga4`, `raw_neto`, `raw_windsor.perf_meta`
- Sources present: Google Ads ✓ (2018-11 → 2026-06, **91 mo**) · GA4 ✓ (2022-08 → 2026-07, **47 mo**) · Neto revenue ✓ (2021-07 → 2026-07, **60 mo**, 279k orders) · Meta ✓ (2025-05 → 2026-06, 13 mo) · Trade Desk ✓ (Windsor `cp`, ~2 mo)
- History: **up to 7.5 years (Google Ads).** Deepest client by far, and the only one with real revenue.
- Freshness: fresh (Google Ads slightly behind at 2026-06-22)
- **Verdict: MMM-READY** — but note the 24mo+ depth is Google Ads + GA4 + revenue; Meta is 13mo and Trade Desk ~2mo, so a *multi-paid-channel* MMM is really Google-Ads-anchored today.

## Storage and cost

Total ≈ **37.83 GB**, of which **~36 GB is `raw_google_ads`** (7+ years of DTS Google Ads history for
City Perfume et al.) and ~2.6 GB is everything else. At `australia-southeast1` active-storage pricing
(~US$0.023/GB-month) that's **≈ US$0.87/month** — storage cost is a rounding error; DTS ingestion is free.
Query cost (not storage) is the thing to watch, but dashboards read small view outputs.

Adding 2 years of off-platform history (planning figure ~100 MB/client × ~10 clients ≈ 1 GB) would add
**~US$0.02/month** — negligible. Storage is not a constraint at this scale; **data *depth and freshness*
are the constraints, not cost.**

## Gaps

- **No client has 24 months across 3+ paid channels.** Trade Desk (the near-universal channel) only
  starts 2026-03-29 in the mirror — 2–4 months everywhere. This is the hard blocker for MMM.
- **Stale sources (>7 days on tables that should be daily):** `raw_windsor.perf_google_ads` (~30d),
  `perf_reddit` (~24d, ResetData's only Reddit source), `perf_ga4`/`perf_ga4_events` (~18d, **VMCH's GA4
  fallback while its DTS is frozen — VMCH web data at risk**), `hubspot_contacts` (~17d, manual load),
  `raw_snowflake.reddit_ads_apac_all` (data ~6d behind).
- **HireRight is stale/between flights** — TTD stopped 2026-06-22, LinkedIn/DV360 ended.
- **Fragmented accounts:** Schneider & HireRight LinkedIn/DV360 are split across currency-suffixed
  accounts (`_USD`/`_AUD`/`_SGD`) that start and stop — continuous per-client history is shorter than the
  raw min/max implies.
- **Empty datasets:** `client_bellshakespeare` (blocked), `client_caltex`, `client_nextsmile` (coming soon),
  `billing_export` (no billing export configured — so no query-cost telemetry in-project).
- **Schema quality:** no per-placement/per-domain/per-site dimension in any raw table (TTD stops at
  campaign×ad_group×creative, Meta at ad×date) — placement-isolation / site-quality analysis is not
  possible without new ingest. Schneider Salesforce leads are all status `New` (CRM-raw, not graded).

## Ready-now recommendations

- **V3 (historical off-platform upload):** no data dependency — applies to all clients equally, and the
  project is a clean slate (no offline tables exist). Start whenever.
- **V4 (real recommendations engine):** recommended pilots by *live channel breadth + conversion signal*,
  not history depth. **ResetData** (Calvin's pick) is confirmed sensible — Google/Meta/Reddit/Trade
  Desk/GA4 + a HubSpot funnel gives the widest cross-channel surface and a real outcome to optimise
  toward, despite only 3–7 months. **Cloudflare** is a good second (TTD+LinkedIn+Reddit+LINE+Salesforce).
  Fix ResetData's stale Reddit + HubSpot feeds first so the engine sees current data.
- **V5 (real ClickUp API):** no data dependency.
- **V6 (Site Quality Index / Jounce):** **blocked on data** — needs Trade Desk / DV360 **domain/site-level**
  impression data, which no raw table currently carries. Requires a new breakdown ingest (or verifying
  whether the Snowflake TTD/DV360 sources expose site columns at origin) before it can be real.
- **V7 (Meridian MMM):** **City Perfume only**, today — it's the sole client with 24mo+ on multiple
  sources (Google Ads 91mo, GA4 47mo, Neto revenue 60mo). Treat it as MMM-anchored-on-Google-Ads until
  Meta/other paid channels accrue depth. **STT GDC** is the next candidate (~2026-2027 once its ~13mo
  paid channels mature to 24mo). All others need 12–21 more months of continuous paid delivery first.
- **For Lex (upstream pipeline) before V4:** (1) re-auth/repair VMCH's GA4 DTS (frozen since ~2026-06-01)
